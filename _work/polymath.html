Polymath Lettering

An excercise under the instruction of Nils Lindstorm, ArtCenter College of Design. Creating an elegant, and beautiful logotype utilizing the craft of hand lettering.

Initial Sketches

Spanning the course of 3 weeks, various sketches and revisions were made, each with increasing level of detail to prepare for final digitalization.

Digitization

Through scanning, I was able to obtain a crisp copy of the design, and continued onward to recreate the entire design again in Illustrator.
