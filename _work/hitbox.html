Hitbox Magazine

Prototype magazine based off the idea of introducing eSports, specifically Counter Strike, to the masses.

Design Ideology

In order to bring the digital experience into a novel physical magazine issue, the dimensions, grid and layout were carefully considered to show as much content as possible, while still maintaining visual clarity.
