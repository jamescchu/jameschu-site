Chanbara Poster and Brochure

A unique challenge to create a poster, yet at the same time, can be folded down into a brochure and used practically. Many design obstacles were faced to achieve this balance, and countless more prototypes needed to be produced to get it right.

Research

To create a poster regarding old Japanese samurai films, one must experience them first. I watched 2 of the most critically aclaimed films on the list, and looked into the many intricacies surrounding Japanese samurai culture.
